Security Freeze

A security freeze, sometimes referred to as a credit freeze or a consumer report freeze, is the best way to help prevent new accounts from being opened in your name. It’s absolutely free to freeze and unfreeze your FactorTrust credit report, and it won’t affect your credit score.

Freeze My Credit

  • Freeze anytime for free
  • Help control who can access your credit information
  • Won’t affect your credit score
  • Can unfreeze anytime for free

Unfreeze My Credit

  • Allow lenders access when you apply for new credit
  • Temporarily lift or permanently remove your freeze
  • Refreeze anytime for free

Manage PIN

  • PINs are there to add security
  • Use it to change manage your freeze online or over the phone
  • Requesting a new PIN is quick and easy
